  • Well, there will be an option in your profile through which you can post your question.
  • There will also be an option in home page itself by which you can easily post your question.
  • You can also edit your question if you feel to change it.
  • You can post your question anonymously also, if you are feeling uncomfortable.
  • You can add necessary pictures along with the question like graphs to make it more clear.
